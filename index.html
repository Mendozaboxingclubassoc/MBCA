WHATH WE ARE AND OUR VALUES

As an association we help people who are experiencing mental problems as well as problems with drug use. We have our own training space and we always prepare ourselves to be the best in the competitions in which we participate. Our work is done under the following values.

RESPECT

There is NO skin-to-skin contact whatsoever, our boxers are only hitting boxing gloves against boxing mitts.

TRUST

We believe that the skills they learn in our classes make them more confident in all other aspects of their lives.

FAIRNESS

We allways ensure to provide an all-inclusive workout program to our boxers as a way to stay both physically and mentally healthy.
